The Gail S. Halvorsen Aviation Education Foundation was formed in 2016 by a group of community-minded volunteers who seek to perpetuate the great legacy of The Candy Bomber story. The Foundation advances aviation education, promotes youth leadership development, enhances community capacity for emergency response, and encourages humanitarian service on macro- and micro-levels.
